The MV Bob Barker is one of Sea Shepherd Global ́s flagships. The photos were taken during a Campaign Break, when the vessel was parked in the Port of Las Palmas de Granaria, Spain for maintenance work (Winter 2016/17). The MV Bob Barker was active in Campaigns over the last years, and still is at present, off the coast of West Africa.
