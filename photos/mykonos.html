Mykonos

Welcome to Mykonos! THE glamour, fashionista island of Greece. Part of the Cycladic Islands, Mykonos is known for its crazy Beach - and Clubparties. Still unknown to many, the island is also a great spot for Scuba Diving Lovers. The water usually is crystal clear, with visibilities above 30m / 100ft not unknown. Mykonos is very rich in the variety and population of marine life, thanks to decades of local Marine Protection Programs.
Add some beautiful reef formations, caverns, swim throughs, and 2 shipwrecks, and your mediterranean Diving Dream becomes true!
