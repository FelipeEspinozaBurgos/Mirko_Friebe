Mahahual

Mahahual was once best described, as one of these ageless, charming fishing villages, in the very south of the Costa Maya (Mexican Caribbean).
Bungalows, Beach Bars, Shrimp Cocktails, and lazy, very lazy afternoons in the shade. Now, this is history. Mahahual changed forever, when the Cruise Ship industry discovered this little, hidden paradise.
When a Cruise Ship is at Dock, Boatloads of tourists flock the town beaches, and the relatively new completed Malecon. But there is a little, easy trick, to stay away from this:
YOU go Diving over the day, and return to town, when the Day Tourists are back on their ship. If you like this, then start reading again from above. Smiley...
